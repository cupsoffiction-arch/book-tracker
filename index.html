How to export from Goodreads
  1. Go to goodreads.com → My Books
  2. Click Import and export (bottom of left sidebar)
  3. Click Export Library and save the CSV
Shelves map automatically: to-read → To Be Read, currently-reading → Reading Now, read → Read & Reviewed.
