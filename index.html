How to export from Goodreads
  1. Go to goodreads.com → My Books
  2. Click Import and export (bottom of left sidebar)
  3. Click Export Library and download the CSV
Shelves map automatically: to-read → To Be Read, currently-reading → Reading Now, read → Read & Reviewed.
If a book from Goodreads already exists on your shelf (e.g. from your physical TBR), the two are merged into one entry — owned flag kept, cover/author filled in where missing, more specific status wins. No duplicate created.

Upload your physical TBR sheet

Any CSV with a title column
Expected columns (any order, flexible names):
title, author, date / published, status, cover

Recognised status values: tbr, arc, reading, review, film, dnf, editing, done — anything else defaults to To Be Read.
Every book in this sheet is flagged as physically owned. If it already exists on your shelf from Goodreads, it is merged and the owned flag added — no duplicate entry is created.
Matching uses normalised title (lowercase, punctuation stripped, leading "the/a/an" ignored), so "The Hobbit" and "Hobbit, The" still match correctly.
